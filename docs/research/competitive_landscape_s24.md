## The 50% WR Crisis

At 50% WR, talent stays flat. The math:
- **55% WR** → +5 talent/day → T200 in ~3 weeks
- **60% WR** → +12 talent/day → T500 in ~5 weeks
- **50% WR** → 0 talent/day → stuck forever

## Three Ceilings

1. **AI Quality (NOW)** — biggest lever. SmartThing proves 6x talent gap at same level.
2. **Stat Budget (L100-200)** — more capital, new chips/weapons unlock.
3. **Level Cap (L200+)** — top 10 is all L301. Time-gated by XP.

## Strategic Implication

The ONLY way to break 50% at our level is better AI. We can't out-stat
opponents with the same capital budget. We can only out-think them.

**Priority**: Study high-talent low-level leeks → reverse-engineer their
strategies → test variants in sim → deploy winner.
